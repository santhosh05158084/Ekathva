About Us

More than an NGO —
a movement of unity.

Ekathva Foundation Society was established in 2020 under the Government of Andhra Pradesh (Registration No. 66 of 2020). Based in Anantapuramu, we work tirelessly to uplift underserved communities across the region.

The word "Ekathva" means oneness — and that spirit drives every program we run. We believe that when communities stand together as one, no challenge is too great.

From distributing blankets in winter cold, to running the landmark LiveInGreen 3K environmental campaign, to coordinating COVID-19 emergency resources 24×7 — we show up wherever we are needed, always.

Our work is built on transparency, compassion, and long-term impact — not one-time charity, but sustained, systemic change alongside the people we serve.

👁 Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual — regardless of caste, gender, or economic standing — has equal access to opportunity, dignity, and a sustainable future. A society bound not by our differences, but by our shared humanity and collective strength.

🎯 Our Mission

To design and implement community-led programs across plantation awareness, quality education, women's empowerment, and disaster preparedness — creating self-reliant communities capable of driving their own sustainable growth and determining their own futures.

Our Journey

A roadmap of purposeful milestones

Every moment on this timeline represents real people helped, real communities strengthened, real lives changed.

2015

ATP Prayanam Launched

Community carpooling initiative connecting Anantapur ↔ Bangalore travellers through trust-based ride sharing. Still running strong today.

2018 · August

LiveInGreen 3K Run

Landmark environmental campaign attracting 2500+ participants. Distributed 2000 plants. Put Anantapuramu on the map for community-led environmental action.

2018 · Mid Year

Stand With Kerala

Kerala Flood Relief fundraising — Anantapuramu's community donated funds, dairy products, sanitation materials, biscuits and clothing for flood-hit families.

2019 · March

Road Safety Awareness Program

Community-wide road safety education initiative targeting accident-prone zones and school areas across Anantapuramu district.

2019 · November

Blankets Distribution Drive

Winter relief — blankets distributed to homeless and vulnerable families across Anantapuramu on November 8th, 2019. Dignity in cold weather.

2019 · December

Books Distribution Program

Quality books and stationery provided to underprivileged students on December 2nd, 2019 — bridging the educational gap one classroom at a time.

2020 · Registered

Ekathva Foundation Officially Registered

Formally registered with the Government of Andhra Pradesh as Ekathva Foundation Society (Registration No. 66 of 2020). A milestone that enables greater impact.

2020 · October 2

Old Age Home Roof Renovation

Complete roof renovation of a local old age home on Gandhi Jayanti — ensuring safety, comfort and dignity for elderly residents of Anantapuramu.

2020–2021

Ekathva COVID-19 Health Line

24×7 emergency coordination: hospital beds, ambulances, tele-consultations, blood and plasma donors, oxygen cylinders, food and medicine delivery — across Andhra Pradesh at the height of the pandemic.

2021 – Present

Ongoing Education & Women Empowerment

Continuous programs across education support, menstrual health awareness, women's skill-building, and community leadership development across Anantapuramu.
